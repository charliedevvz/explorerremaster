Hero

A Hero wields a Sword or an Axe and focuses on gaining Combo orbs, boosting their damage and allowing for use of special attacks to debuff their enemies.

Paladin

A Paladin wields a Sword or a Mace and uses Elemental properties to boost their attacks and reduce damage taken from enemies.

Dark Knight

A Dark Knight wields a Spear or a Polearm and fights alongside the Evil Eye, a spirit of darkness that supports allies and pushes the Dark Knight's power to its limits.
